Lasagna

Our delicious lasagna finished!

Today we will prepare a dish that everyone will love. A classic that pretty much everyone knows about... Lasagna!

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
